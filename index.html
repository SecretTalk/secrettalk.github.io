Why Use This

Because you don't trust anybody to store your conversations, you're a skeptical paranoid crypto-anarchist who doesn't wanna rely on any messaging platforms. Not even Snowden's recommended "Signal"! Yes, not even that.

Open Source

SecretTalk's code is completely open sourse and can be forked and modified to fit your application needs.

Secured

Storing conversations locally doesn't mean that they won't be encrypted, it means that they won't be accessed to be decrypted by any 3rd parties.

Ruby

Ruby comes pre-installed on almost every UNIX operating system.

Almost Zero Configuration

You don't need to have any database technology to be installed. All you need to do is clone, setup, and run!

Admin Dashboard

Your messaging app is invite-based, create and invite new users without any coding hustles through a user friendly UI.

Scan To Join

All you need to have the app installed on devices you would like to have connected to your server, is to send them a QR code to scan, and then they'll be able to install a PWA with one click.
